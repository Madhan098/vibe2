How It Works

Simple, powerful, and designed for developers

01

Start Coding (No Upload Needed!)

For Beginners: Just start coding! Enable Beginner Mode in the editor and CodeMind learns YOUR style from every function you write. Choose from multiple style options (Concise, Descriptive, Professional) and watch it learn your preferences. For Experienced Developers: Upload local files or connect your GitHub account. CodeMind analyzes files in Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, and 20+ other languages.

02

Extract Your Style DNA

For Beginners: CodeMind learns incrementally! After 5-10 interactions, it knows your style preferences. Each choice you make (concise vs descriptive, documented vs minimal) builds your unique profile. For Experienced Developers: Our proprietary algorithm analyzes your coding patterns: naming conventions, documentation style, error handling, code structure, and more. Get a comprehensive style profile that represents YOUR unique coding fingerprint.

03

Get AI Suggestions

For Beginners: See 2-3 style options for every function you write. Click the one that feels right, and CodeMind learns your preference. It grows WITH you as you learn! For Experienced Developers: As you code, CodeMind provides real-time suggestions that match YOUR style. Context-aware improvements with teaching explanations help you understand why changes are better. Accept or reject to help CodeMind learn your preferences.

04

Generate Code in Your Style

Ask CodeMind to generate code, and it will create solutions matching your detected style. Whether you need a function, class, or complete module, CodeMind writes code that looks like YOU wrote it. Even if you've never coded before, CodeMind learns YOUR style!
